University of Rochester

Rochester, NY

Undergraduate Researcher, Senior Thesis on Galaxy Evolution

04/2024 – 05/2025

Advisors: Prof. Segev BenZvi, Prof. Kelly Douglass

  • Classified ~18M galaxies from DESI Year 1 into cosmic environments using V2 and VoidFinder catalogs; prepared datasets for statistical analysis.
  • Performed nonparametric Bayesian inference with Pólya tree priors to test for differences in the distribution of galaxy properties (e.g., stellar mass, color) across galaxy environments.
  • Interpreted results indicating enhanced star formation and delayed quenching in underdense regions; presented statistical findings at two departmental symposia (Aug 2024, May 2025).
  • Authored a 30-page thesis synthesizing cosmological context with statistical modeling to draw conclusions about galaxy evolution. Available via this link.
